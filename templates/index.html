How To Use

Create Account

Create an account with Wallets.africa. A mail will be sent to you for email verification, click on the mail to verify your account.

fingerprint

Get Keys

Log into your Wallets.africa account. Go to Settings > Developer Keys then request for keys.

payment

Buy Airtime

After you have gotten your keys, use the details to purchase airtime by clicking the button above and filling the form accordingly. Airtime purchase will be charged from your Wallets.africa wallet.
